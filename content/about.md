# About Me

I'm a **Power Electronics Engineer** and **Automation Enthusiast** from Switzerland, passionate about making electrons dance in perfect formation!

## Background

My journey started at **Politecnico di Torino** where I earned my B.Sc. (2006) and M.Sc. (2008) in Electrical Engineering. Since then, I've been on a mission to bridge the gap between theory and practice, one circuit at a time.

Currently working on automotive power electronics, diving deep into Python & AI, and collaborating on GeckoCIRCUIT software. Because why choose between hardware and software when you can make both work together?

> *"Leave the world in a better shape than what you have found"* - this isn't just my motto, it's my daily mission through knowledge sharing and community building.
